My Business Idea

The business that I would like to open one day would be a gun range. As someone who enjoys shooting and has friends that also do, we have talked about how cool it would be to open our own range. My family has some property out in Iowa and I think it would be amazing to one day turn it into a range. We would have one area set for outdoor long range shooting and another indoor area for shorter range weapons. Another outdoor area could be for things like shotguns and other guns that may make a mess.
